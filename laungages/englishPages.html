How much does it cost to get a tattoo?

On average, getting a tattoo at the OldBoy barbershop will cost from 2,000 rubles. But the final value of the price of a tattoo depends on many factors. It is important to consider: size, color, tattoo style, direction, place for application In other words, the cost of the service is discussed individually with the tattoo artist for a free consultation. He will certainly advise you on all types of tattoo art, help you decide on a stylistic direction, professionally select a color and, if you are in doubt about a place for a future tattoo, he will give you a tip from his experience. Deciding on a tattoo is a bold act, but it must be deliberate and coordinated. with your inner self. There is also another important aspect: the sanitary standards of the tattoo parlor, safety, sterility, and the portfolio of the artist you are referring to. In the OldBoy barbershop network, there are tattoo parlors where the masters go through tough competition and strict selection in terms of portfolio and skills, then undergo mandatory certification and certification. And only then they are accepted for work in a barbershop. Tattoo rooms are equipped with the latest technologies, comply with all sanitary and hygienic standards and are licensed. In their work, the craftsmen use modern equipment, high-quality paints and disposable consumables. Some of our tattoo artists have over 10 years of experience. The advantage of each of them is a creative approach to business, a significant portfolio and not a hundred satisfied clients who come back to us again and again. The tattoo artist is ready to get tattoos in traditional and old school techniques, including impressive Japanese tattoos, biomechanics and tribal, and also numerous exotic ornaments (Maori, Polynesia, etc.), inscriptions in various fonts, tattoos in the style of realism, or, conversely, dot and linework. OldBoy Barbershop has a huge database of sketches and all kinds of fonts, allowing you to choose any style that appeals to you. Also, the master will help to make an individual and unique sketch of your future tattoo.
