About

    Sparties Barbershop is the place to go when you need a new look, done right. Our barbers are dedicated to making sure you have a hairstyle you can proudly show off.
    We have a team of 5 licensed and accredited barbers who have a combined 50 years of experience. Our staff is great group of talented professionals, and we are always ready to exceed your expectations.
    When you are ready to take your hairstyle to the next level, drop by our barber shop in East Lansing, MI.

Walk-Ins Welcome

Walk-ins are welcome all day. Monday thru Friday from 9am to 7pm and from 9am to 3pm on Saturday. When you come in the front door, there is a Ipad sign-in sheet. Please sign in on the Ipad to secure your spot in line.
